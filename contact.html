Frequently Asked Questions

What are the admission requirements?

+

Admission requirements vary by program. Generally, you'll need a completed application form, transcripts from previous studies, a statement of purpose, and proof of language proficiency for programs taught in English or French. Please check the specific requirements for your program of interest.

Do you offer financial aid or scholarships?

+

Yes, we offer various financial aid options and scholarships for eligible students. These include merit-based scholarships, need-based financial aid, and payment plans. Contact our financial aid office at financialaid@city-ihl.ca for more information.

Can I visit the campus before applying?

+

Absolutely! We encourage prospective students to visit our campus. You can schedule a campus tour by selecting 'Campus Visit' in the contact form above, or by contacting our admissions office directly. We offer both in-person and virtual tour options.

What is the application deadline?

+

Application deadlines vary by program and intake period. Most programs have rolling admissions, but we recommend applying at least 3 months before your intended start date to allow time for processing. Check the specific program page or contact admissions for exact deadlines.
